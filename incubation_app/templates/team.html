Our Team & Mentors

Meet the experienced team and mentors at AITAM RISE, dedicated to nurturing the next generation of innovators and entrepreneurs.

Core Team

Dr. Anya Sharma

Director

Dr. Sharma leads AITAM RISE with over 15 years of experience in startup incubation and technology commercialization. She holds a PhD in Engineering and has mentored numerous successful startups.

Mr. Rohan Verma

Program Manager

Mr. Verma manages the day-to-day operations of the incubator, ensuring smooth program delivery and resource allocation. He has a background in business administration and a passion for supporting early-stage ventures.

Ms. Priya Kapoor

Community Lead

Ms. Kapoor fosters a vibrant community within the incubator, organizing events, workshops, and networking opportunities. She has a degree in communications and a knack for connecting people.

Mentors

Mr. Vikram Singh

Technology Advisor

Mr. Singh provides technical guidance to startups, leveraging his 20+ years of experience in software development and IT consulting. He has a Master's in Computer Science and has worked with several Fortune 500 companies.

Ms. Neha Reddy

Marketing Strategist

Ms. Reddy specializes in marketing and branding, helping startups define their target audience and develop effective marketing strategies. She has an MBA and has worked with both startups and established brands.

Mr. Arjun Patel

Financial Consultant

Mr. Patel offers financial advice to startups, assisting with budgeting, fundraising, and financial planning. He is a certified financial analyst with over 10 years of experience in the financial industry.

Ms. Divya Iyer

Legal Counsel

Ms. Iyer provides legal support to startups, covering areas such as intellectual property, contracts, and regulatory compliance. She is a licensed attorney with a focus on business law and startup advising.
